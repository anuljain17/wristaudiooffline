Quick Troubleshooting

  • Ensure iPhone and Apple Watch are paired and both apps are installed.
  • Keep both apps open for first transfer setup.
  • Use supported formats: MP3, AAC/M4A, or WAV.
  • If playback route fails on watch, check Bluetooth route or enable speaker fallback in watch settings.
  • If transfer stalls, restart both apps and retry transfer.
